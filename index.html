About

Helix Professional Services connects firms to an industry experienced team to assist in everything from short-term projects to long-term strategy during a structured 6-week sprint guided by our proprietary Double Helix framework. After finding a project to collaborate on, Helix hand selects project teams to best fit the client's needs from skill verticals designated by industry or work type.

Helix is a far more affordable professional services option for startups and scaling businesses looking for advice on anything from a day-to-day project to an organization defining strategy. Helix also differentiates on quality for similarly priced freelance work. Online platforms like Canduit fail to vet their laborers extensively and Consulting Clubs (like the ones lots of our staff are part of) often serves as a person's first real project experience during their Freshman or Sophomore year. Therefore, Helix serves a missing middle in the professional services market. One that is affordable enough to consult on a regular basis, but experienced enough to be worth consulting before any major business decision.
